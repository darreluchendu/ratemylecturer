About


Students

This project is made to help those students who would like to know more about his/her lecturer of a class they're taking, in order to know what to expect before coming to class. The lecturer's policy on assignments, how they mark their exam papers. These are all necessary for students to survive and thrive in the class.

Lecturers

This project aims to paint a better picture for lecturers to gauge how their material appeals to the students taking their course. RateMyLecturer would allow lecturers to see what the students are thinking about them and hopefully, encourage the lecturers to improve further their methods of\ teaching.
